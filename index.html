Origins and Creation

The Les Paul was designed in the 1950s by the legendary guitarist Les Paul in collaboration with Gibson. It was introduced to the market in 1952, quickly becoming one of the most iconic electric guitars of all time.

Evolution Over the Years

Over the decades, the Les Paul has undergone various modifications and improvements, such as the introduction of the Les Paul Standard in 1958 with humbucker pickups, which redefined its sound.

Cultural Impact

Used by legendary musicians like Jimmy Page, Slash, and Eric Clapton, the Les Paul has left an indelible mark on the history of rock and other music genres.
